Privacy Policy

The "We Don't Actually Collect Anything" Section

Since this is just a demo site, we're not actually collecting any of your data. That email form at the end? It goes nowhere. It's like shouting into the void, except the void has a nice green button.

What we collect: Absolutely nothing. Nada. Zilch. Your secrets are safe because we're not even listening.

Weather Data (The Fun Stuff)

We do make real API calls to Open-Meteo to get historical weather data. This is probably the only real thing on this entire site! We use this data to calculate hilariously specific insurance premiums for golf days that happened 30 years ago.

Fun fact: We can tell you if it was raining in Ballybunion on March 15, 1987 at 2 PM. Why? Because we can, and it's surprisingly entertaining.

Cookies (The Digital Kind)

We don't use cookies. Not because we're privacy champions, but because this is a simple demo and we were too lazy to implement them. Your browser might store some stuff locally because that's what browsers do, but we're not involved in that relationship.

If you want actual cookies while browsing this site, we recommend chocolate chip. They pair well with the disappointment of realizing you can't actually buy weather insurance for your golf game.

Third-Party Services

Open-Meteo API: We use their weather service to get historical data. They're the real MVPs here. They probably have their own privacy policy that's much more serious than this one.

Tailwind CSS: We load this from a CDN because we like pretty buttons. They might log that you loaded their CSS file. We can't control what they do, but we trust them because their gradients are *chef's kiss*.

Unsplash: That gorgeous golf course image? It's from Unsplash. They might know you looked at it. Again, not our circus, not our monkeys.
